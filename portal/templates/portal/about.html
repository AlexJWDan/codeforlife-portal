Ocado Technology

On the surface, Ocado Technology is the world’s largest online-only grocery retailer. Customers use our award winning mobile applications and website to place their orders, which are packed in our world–class automated warehouses, and delivered in one hour time slots.

Look under the surface however, and you’ll unearth a dynamic technology company that has the look, feel and culture of an innovative software start-up. That’s because we develop all the software that powers Ocado in-house.

We have developed the Code for Life initiative to help support primary school teachers deliver the new computer science curriculum with the aim of equipping every child in the country with the “coding survival skills” that will enable them to flourish in an increasingly digital world.
